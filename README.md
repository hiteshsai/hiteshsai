## 💫 About Me

I'm a Security Product Manager at Microsoft, shaping the strategy, roadmap, and customer experience for security products that protect organizations at global scale. I partner closely with engineering, design, research, and GTM teams to turn customer pain points into shipped capabilities — across cloud security, threat protection, identity, and compliance.

- 🔭 Currently driving product strategy for cloud security and zero-trust experiences
- 🧭 Translating customer insights and threat trends into a clear product vision and roadmap
- 🤝 Partnering with engineering, design, and field teams to ship and scale impactful features
- 🎯 Focused on outcomes: adoption, customer trust, time-to-value, and measurable security uplift

<br/>

## 🌟 What I Focus On as a PM

<div align="center">

### Core Areas

🧭 **Product Strategy** | 🗺️ **Roadmap & Prioritization** | 🔍 **Customer Discovery** | 📈 **Outcomes & Metrics**

</div>

- 🎯 **Vision & Strategy** — Defining the "why" and "what" for security products serving enterprise customers
- 🗣️ **Customer Discovery** — Running interviews, design partnerships, and listening systems with CISOs, SOC leads, and security architects
- 🧩 **Cross-Functional Leadership** — Aligning engineering, design, research, marketing, sales, and partners on a shared plan
- 📊 **Data-Informed Decisions** — Using telemetry, funnels, and qualitative insight to prioritize the right bets
- 🚀 **Launch & GTM** — Partnering on positioning, pricing, docs, enablement, and go-to-market for new capabilities
- 🛡️ **Security Domain Depth** — Bringing fluency in cloud security, identity, SIEM/XDR, and zero-trust to product decisions
